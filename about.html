About Us

We Take Robotics Seriously

Our Team PUPR Vex Robotics is composed by a diverse group of students from
the Polytechnic University of Puerto Rico. We have students from different
branches of engineering. We are very passionate engineering students that love
robotics and learning something new every day about STEM. Our main goal as
a team is to complete and exceed our expectation for our designs and
programs, acieving a top position or, even better, winning. We invite you to go
through our website to meet our team and more. We want you to be part of
our experience.

We Take Robotics To
The Next Step

Our Team has experience in making 3D modeling robots in Autodesk and
VR programing. By having this experience in the COVID-19 pandemic
our problem was that we couldn’t meet up to build our robots but
thanks to Autodesk we didn’t have a problem with that because we
could build 3D modeling robots in Autocad Inventor. As well as
programming, thanks to the Vex Robotics company we were able to
program our robots in VR and see the reaction of the robots. With
these programs, we are able to take robotics to the next steps and
help us guide us to success.
