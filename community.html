Community Outreach

Our group is enriched by each of its members and its individual
capabilities; each of them help our community differently.

WORKSHOP

We had the opportunity to meet a couple of students from the international School of Programming in Arroyo, Puerto Rico. The VEX Team collaborated with AEMIM (a PUPR student organization) where we had the chance to taught them about how a rocket works and how the VEX system works in order to motivate them into joining this amazing community.

TUTORING

Our team members are knowledgeable in subjects like
thermodynamics, mathematics and physics. They give
tutoring to other students, and to new members.

MENTORSHIP

We have helped many local VEX teams through mentorship
and teaching. This has helped us develop leadership skills
for the future.

ORGANIZING

Our team members have participated in organizing the VEX
High School League and they participated as judges in high
school matches.

AUTODESK

We help locals with making robotics in Autodesk Inventor so
they can have the skills of making 3D modeling.
