THIS. IS. EBRT.

We are a mixed group composed by students of all Engineering
disciplines. Our university is right in the Capital of Puerto Rico, San
Juan. We are part of one of the many robotics teams is our 100x35
island.

Our Logo

Our university mascot is a beaver. Yes, a beaver! We wanted to give a little twist
to out traditional beaver for one with robotic elements to honor our team.
The official colors of our university are royal blue and yellow, which is why our logo
has those colors. The Polytechnic University is our main sponsor, so we want to
represent them with respect and pride. It’s important to mention that this logo
was created by one of our member’s father. We loved it!
